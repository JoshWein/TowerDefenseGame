Instructions

Objective: Stop the waves of enemies from reaching your base. You lose if your health at the top left reaches zero. You win if you can survive every wave.

You can open and close the sidebar by clicking on the arrow attached to the top of the sidebar.

Buying Towers: Choose a tower from the sidebar and place it on the map to buy it.

Selling/Upgrading Towers: Select a tower on the map and then scroll to the bottom of the sidebar to either sell or upgrade it.
