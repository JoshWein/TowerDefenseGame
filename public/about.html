International Klein Blue Life

International Klein Blue Life is a Tower Defense game inspired by Conway's Game of Life.
In IKB Life, you are tasked with the responsibility of protecting Conway's Kingdom from incoming invaders.
Place and upgrade an army of Cellular Automata Towers onto puzzling terrains to strategically fend off the foes.
